# News Article Categorisation — Problem Statement

## Business Context
News aggregators and content platforms (Google News, RSS readers) need to automatically tag millions of articles per day into topic categories — sports, politics, tech, etc. — for personalised feeds.

## Problem Type
Multi-class text classification (20 categories). Supervised learning.

## Success Metrics
- **Accuracy** ≥ 85% on test set
- **Macro F1** ≥ 0.80
- **Inference time** < 10 ms per article
