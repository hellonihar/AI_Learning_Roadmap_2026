# The NovaTech AI Initiative

A fictional yet realistic 2-year journey of enterprise AI adoption — told through the eyes of a team of architects, senior engineers, and business analysts navigating failures, course corrections, and eventual success.

## Characters

| Name | Role | Archetype |
|------|------|-----------|
| Anika Sharma | Lead AI Architect | Visionary, technically deep, patient |
| Raj Mehta | Senior ML Engineer | Pragmatic, cynical, brilliant coder |
| Elena Vasquez | Senior MLOps Engineer | Reliability-obsessed, cautious |
| David Chen | Business Analyst | Bridge between tech & business |
| Marcus Johnson | VP of AI | Executive sponsor, ROI-focused |

## Phases

| Phase | Chapters | Timeline | Summary |
|-------|----------|----------|---------|
| 1 — The Honeymoon | 1-3 | Months 1-3 | Grand ambition meets reality |
| 2 — The Fall | 4-7 | Months 3-6 | Production disaster forces reckoning |

| 6 — The Horizon | 19-20 | Month 20+ | Lessons learned, roadmap ahead |

## Reading Order

Start from **Chapter 1** and read through. Each chapter builds on the previous. Technical concepts are introduced naturally through the story — no infodumps, just engineering dialogue and real dilemmas.
